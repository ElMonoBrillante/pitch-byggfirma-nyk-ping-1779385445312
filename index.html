About us

Well-Established Builders in Nyköping

We are a well-renowned construction company with qualified personnel who are happy to help you with your building project, large or small. With extensive experience in construction and renovation, we deliver quality workmanship and friendly service. Contact us to discuss your project and receive a free quote.

Why choose us

  • Extensive Network of Craftsmen We have a large network of skilled tradespeople including plumbers, tilers, HVAC installers, and sheet metal workers. This means we can assist you with the entire project, so you don't need to hire multiple companies.
  • ROT-avdrag Eligible When you hire us as a private individual, you may be entitled to ROT-avdrag, which can reduce your labor costs to as little as 70% of the total. Contact us for a consultation to learn more.
  • Serving All of Södermanland We work throughout Sörmland, including Oppeby, Harg, Brandholmen, Nyköping, Ekensberg, Bryngelstorp, and Isaksdal. Local expertise with broad service coverage.
  • Free Quote & Online Booking We offer free, no-obligation quotes for your project. You can book a consultation online 24/7 at your convenience.
